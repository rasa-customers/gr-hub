Q. If I have already left the restaurant, and the order has missing items, will I still get full delivery credit for this order?
A. Yes. If the issue is not caused by the driver, you’ll receive full delivery credit and your pay will not be impacted.

Q. Do I need to go back to the restaurant for missing items?
A. No. If you’ve already left the restaurant, do not return. Proceed with delivery and support will handle refunds or credits.

Q. What should I do if I notice an issue before leaving the restaurant?
A. Stay at the location and report the issue. You may be asked to wait briefly while the restaurant corrects the order.

Q. Do I need to contact the customer myself about missing items?
A. No. Support will notify the customer and handle any refunds or credits automatically.
